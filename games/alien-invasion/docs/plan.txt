Objective: Score as many points as possible and beat high score.

Setup:
	- Player is a ship that can move left and right on bottom of screen
	  using left and right arrow keys. 
	- Player can shoot bullets with spacebar.
	- Fleet of target aliens cover the rest of the screen and moves down
	  toward the player ship.
	- Every new fleet of aliens is faster than the previous fleet. 
	- Player has three ships.
